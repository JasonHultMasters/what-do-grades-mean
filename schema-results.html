What is "Pairwise Ranking"? Each time someone took the survey, they compared schemas head-to-head — picking which one they preferred in each matchup. We use an Elo scoring system (the same method used to rank chess players) to turn those picks into an overall ranking. Beating a highly-ranked schema earns more points than beating a lower-ranked one. The scores here reflect the collective preference of everyone who has participated so far.
