Where these schemas came from

A little background before you dive in.

At a recent Academic Committee meeting, members were asked to jot down what each letter grade means to them. Specifically: "What quality of work does an A describe? A B? A C?" — and so on through F.

We collected 18 responses. We then used an AI language model to condense those responses into 8 distinct grading schemas. Each one preserves verbatim language from the original responses, but is a mashup — edited for directness and clarity.

Your job is simple: compare them side by side and tell us which ones resonate. There are no right answers.
